**Figure 3. Rocking-curve width identifies process windows that sharpen AlN c-axis texture.** AlN(0002) rocking-curve full width at half maximum (FWHM, Δω) for wurtzite AlN films grown on oxynitride-treated SiO₂ under the indicated chamber-conditioning and plasma recipes. Bars report fitted FWHM values; horizontal error bars denote fit uncertainty where available. Lower FWHM corresponds to sharper out-of-plane c-axis texture. In the 60-min growth series, lower-pressure pre-sputtering without a 350 °C soak gave the narrowest measured rocking curve (run #29, Δω = 1.09°). The hatched run #27 entry is retained for transparency but excluded from trend interpretation because the rocking-curve fit was unreliable.
